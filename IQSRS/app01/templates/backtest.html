Strategy Library

MACD

MACD uses the convergence and separation between the short-term and the long-term exponential moving average of closing prices to determine the timing of buying and selling. This strategy makes more signal requirements and restrictions to avoid false signals, which makes it a good choice for users.

RBreaker

The RBreaker combines trend and reversal trading which provide more trading opportunities. Not only can the trend market be captured to get large profits, but also when the market trend reverses, it can take the initiative to make profit and to track the trend reverse..

Boll Channel

Boll Channel The Boll Channel strategy is based on the dynamic support and resistance of the Bollinger band line. When the line tilts up, buying opportunities can be sought. Otherwise, selling opportunities can be found. This makes the strategy has the advantages of flexibility, intuitiveness and tendency.

LSTM

LSTM is an improved version of RNN which inherits the accuracy of deep learning and effectively solve the problems of gradient explosion and gradient loss. Current input and succession of previous period can be splicing trained into four values, which allows the model to forget the unimportant thing and select important memory which pass on to the next period.
